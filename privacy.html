Privacy Policy

The Company may collect the following information:

The Company will not provide personal information to third parties without the user's consent, except as required by law. However, information may be provided to external service providers (payment processors, cloud services, analysis tools, etc.) within the scope necessary for service provision.

4. Data Management

The Company manages personal information appropriately and takes reasonable measures to prevent leakage, falsification, and unauthorized access.

5. Retention Period

Personal information is stored only for the period necessary to achieve the purpose of use and will be deleted appropriately when it is no longer required.

6. User Rights

Users may request disclosure, correction, or deletion of their own personal information.

7. Use by Minors

If a minor uses the Services, consent from a parent or guardian is required.

8. International Operations

The Services may be used outside of Japan. In such cases, the user's information may be processed on servers located outside of the user's country.

9. Revisions

This policy may be revised as necessary to comply with legal changes or service updates.
